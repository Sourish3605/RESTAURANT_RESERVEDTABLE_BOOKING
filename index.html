About DineEase

Experience refined buffet dining elevated with style and serenity. Nestled on the rooftop of Sheraton Dhaka in Banani, this multi-cuisine gem offers stunning panoramic views complemented by lush greenery and al-fresco seating

Indulge in a sumptuous melange of global flavors—from sizzling Turkish kebabs and classic Indian curries to delicate Japanese sushi, Asian stir-fries, continental delights, and fresh seafood specialties.

Our team of talented chefs brings years of experience from around the world, creating a menu that blends traditional techniques with innovative flavors.

The atmosphere is warm and inviting, with thoughtfully landscaped spaces perfect for cozy dinners, celebratory gatherings, or a memorable evening with loved ones.
